- **X-ray crystallography game** — an interactive teaching how structure is
  recovered from diffraction: aim X-rays at a crystal, read the resulting
  diffraction pattern, and reconstruct the molecule from the spots. Ties into the
  Perutz thread in the hemoglobin essay (twenty years of firing X-rays at
  hemoglobin crystals to get the first 3D model).
